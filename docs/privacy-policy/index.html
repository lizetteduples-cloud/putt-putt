Privacy Policy - Putt-Putt!

Effective date: 6 July 2026

The short version: Putt-Putt! collects no personal data at all. Everything you enter stays on your device. There are no ads, no accounts, no analytics, and no tracking of any kind.

What the app stores

Putt-Putt! is a mini golf scorecard. Player names, emojis, colours, scores, game history, and statistics are saved only in the app's local storage on your own device. This data is never transmitted to us or to anyone else. Deleting the app or using the in-app "Clear history" button permanently removes it.

What the app does not do

  • No personal information is collected, stored, or shared by the developer.
  • No account or sign-in is required or offered.
  • No advertising, analytics, or tracking libraries are included.
  • No access to your contacts, location, camera, microphone, or files.

Internet use

The app works fully offline. When an internet connection is available, it may load its two display fonts, Fredoka and Quicksand, from Google Fonts. This involves a standard web request to Google's servers, which may expose your IP address to Google, as any web request does. No app data, names, or scores are ever included in that request. Google's privacy policy is available at policies.google.com/privacy.

Sharing results

The "Share podium" button creates an image of your final scores on your device and hands it to your phone's standard share menu. Where it goes from there, such as WhatsApp, is entirely your choice and is handled by the app you share it to.

Children

Putt-Putt! is a family app suitable for all ages. Because it collects no data from anyone, it collects no data from children.

Changes to this policy

If a future version of the app ever changes how data is handled, this page will be updated and the effective date above will change.
